How Does it Work?

Every dollar you spend, you get a victory point. You can redeem victory points for in-store credit only (they cannot be used online but can be acquired online).

A phone number and email are required for your membership. When you check out in-store and have a membership, tell the cashier your name when they ask if you have one.

IMPORTANT: Trading in a product does not get you victory points. Only in-store and online purchases qualify. Any points that you have acquired can not be converted into cash.

*Victory Point Rewards

100 Victory Points: $10

250 VP: $27

500 VP: $55

1000 VP: $110

2000 VP: $235

*The rewards above are the only redeemable options. If you are between, below, or above reward values, you can't get that amount. Only the specified amounts can be rewarded.
