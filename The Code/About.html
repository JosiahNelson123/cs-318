About End Mode Games

Our History

We have been selling everything video game-related since 2021. While opening during the pandemic was tricky, we still managed to thrive due to the rise in popularity of gaming. In Eau Claire, we noticed that not many places offer trade-ins or the ability to sell your used products to the store. So, we decided to step in. Today, we still sell used and new products, as well as giving you the chance to sell your products for cash or store credit.

Our Goal

We hope to provide you with all of your gaming dreams at the best prices. From the newest Xboxes and Play Stations to Gameboys and top-of-the-line gaming PCs, we have it all!
